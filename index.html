National Renewable Energy Trends

This chart illustrates the inverse relationship between Australia's renewable electricity share (%) and electricity-sector emissions (Mt CO₂-e) from 2010 to 2025. As renewable generation increases over time, total emissions decline visually reinforcing the national shift toward cleaner energy. The dual-axis layout allows both metrics to be compared on a single timeline, clearly communicating long-term decarbonisation progress.
